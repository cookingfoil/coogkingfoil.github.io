About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Graduate School of Culture Technology at KAIST and my advisor is Prof. Byungjoo Lee. I received my M.S. degree in Culture Technology from KAIST in 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Byungjoo Lee. I worked for SK Hynix as a R&D researcher from 2015 to 2017. I received my B.S. degree in Material Science and Engineering from KAIST in 2015.

I am an enthusiastic researcher in the field of Human-Computer Interaction. My research direction is to apply mathematical models to measure and quantify human performance in the FPS (First-Person Shooting) games, or in the everyday pointing task with a computer mouse. I am a lover of all kinds of adventure and derive great pleasure from playing games 🎮, driving 🚗, golf ⛳ and talking with inspiring people 🕊.
