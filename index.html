I am currently a Ph.D. student at Graduate School of Culture Technology at KAIST and my advisor is Prof. Byungjoo Lee. I received my M.S. degree in Culture Technology from KAIST in 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Byungjoo Lee. I spent two years as a R&D researcher at SK Hynix. I received my B.S. degree in Material Science and Engineering from KAIST in 2015. I am an enthusiastic researcher in the field of Human-Computer Interaction and my research direction is to apply mathematical models to measure and quantify human performance in the everyday pointing task with a computer mouse of in the FPS(First-Person Shooting) games.. I am a lover of adventure and derive great pleasure from playing computer games, driving and talking with inspiring people.


[Research topic]
1) Human-Computer Interaction
2) Mathematical modeling to measure and quantify the human performance in pointing task
3) Analyzing the player's behavior related to aiming in FPS(First-Person Shooting) game
